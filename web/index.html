Setup in under a minute

How it works.

  1. 1

    Sign in on iPhone

    Open Slatly on your iPhone, enter your Somfy / TaHoma / Connexoon account. They never leave your device unencrypted.

  2. 2

    iCloud Keychain syncs to Watch

    Credentials travel through iCloud Keychain to your paired Apple Watch, usually within seconds. No extra pairing required.

  3. 3

    Leave the phone behind

    From here on, the watch talks to the Somfy cloud directly over LTE or Wi-Fi, which then relays commands to your TaHoma box at home. Free your hands; the blinds answer to your wrist.

Quick facts.

Good to know

Frequently asked.

Which blinds work?

Any motorised exterior venetian blind paired with a Somfy TaHoma, Connexoon or Cozytouch box — Somfy itself, plus io-homecontrol / RTS partner brands (Profalux, Asio, Eltako and others your box already supports). If they show up in the Somfy / TaHoma app, Slatly will see them.

Do I need an iPhone nearby?

Only the first time, to sign in. After credentials sync via iCloud Keychain (or via the built-in Watch hand-off), the Watch app runs fully standalone over LTE or Wi-Fi.

Do I need extra hardware?

No new hardware. Slatly reuses the Somfy TaHoma, Connexoon or Cozytouch box that already controls your blinds. No bridge, no second hub.

Where are my credentials stored?

In Apple's iCloud Keychain, end-to-end encrypted under your Apple ID. The app itself never sends them to any third party, only to Somfy's official OAuth endpoint.

Is it open source?

Yes. The Swift code, the OverkizKit library, and this website are all on GitHub. Contributions and translations are welcome.

Is it on the App Store?

Yes — Slatly is live on the App Store for $0.99 / a few crowns. One-time purchase, no subscription.

Can I control Somfy blinds from Apple Watch without the iPhone?

Yes, that's exactly what Slatly is built for. After a one-time iPhone sign-in, the Watch app runs fully standalone over LTE or Wi-Fi. Your iPhone can be downstairs, off, or out of Bluetooth range.

What Apple Watch apps work with Somfy TaHoma?

Slatly is purpose-built for the Somfy ExteriorVenetianBlind class on Connexoon, TaHoma and Cozytouch boxes — and it's the only third-party watchOS app that drives them standalone, without proxying every command through the iPhone.

How is Slatly different from the official Somfy app?

Slatly focuses entirely on the Apple Watch experience: real Crown-driven slat tilt, drag-to-closure gestures and standalone operation without the iPhone. The Somfy app remains useful for box setup and firmware updates; Slatly is the daily driver from your wrist.
